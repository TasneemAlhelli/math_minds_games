Math Maze

Solve mathematical equations at varying difficulty levels to earn maximum points.

  1. Levels:
    • The game has three difficulty levels: Easy, Intermediate, Advance
  2. Timer:
    • The game starts with a 10-seconds timer
    • Successfully answering a question adds 5 seconds to the timer
  3. Scoring:
    • Answering within 5 seconds
      • Easy level +5 points
      • Intermediate level +10 points
      • Advance level +15 points
    • Answering in more than 5 seconds +3 points
  4. Incorrect Answers:
    • A wrong answer terminates the game
